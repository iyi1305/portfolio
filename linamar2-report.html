Co-op Work Term Report

Business Analyst Co-op | Linamar Corporation

Location: Guelph, Ontario | Term Length: 4 Months

Introduction

This website documents my four-month co-op work term as a Business Analyst at Linamar Corporation. During this term, I worked with operational and business data to support reporting, analysis, and data-driven decision-making across the organization.

The goal of this report is to highlight the skills I developed, the technologies I worked with, and the learning outcomes achieved throughout the work term. A key takeaway from this experience was learning how to transform raw data into accurate, meaningful insights that stakeholders rely on for operational and business decisions.

About the Employer

Linamar Corporation is a global manufacturing company serving industries such as automotive, mobility, and industrial solutions. The organization operates across multiple countries and utilizes a vertically integrated structure that allows data from operations to flow directly into reporting and decision-making systems.

From a computing and analytics perspective, Linamar places strong emphasis on business intelligence, reporting systems, and internal data platforms. This environment provided valuable hands-on experience applying data analysis, problem-solving, and technical skills in a real-world business setting.

Work Term Goals

At the beginning of the work term, I established the following learning goals:

  • Develop a strong understanding of Linamar’s business processes and data flow
  • Strengthen data analysis skills using Excel, SQL, and Power BI
  • Improve data accuracy through structured analysis and validation
  • Create clear and reusable documentation for reports and workflows
  • Enhance existing dashboards and reports to better support stakeholders
  • Build foundational proficiency in VB.NET for internal tools and automation
  • Identify and implement improvements to reports or workflows

Goal Reflection: I successfully achieved most of these goals during the work term. I gained hands-on experience analyzing large datasets, maintaining dashboards used by stakeholders, and improving reporting clarity and efficiency.

While my VB.NET experience remained at a foundational level, I developed a strong understanding of how automation and internal tools support reporting processes, providing a solid base for future technical growth.

Job Description

As a Business Analyst Co-op, my role focused on supporting business operations through data analysis, reporting, and documentation. My responsibilities involved working closely with both data and stakeholders.

  • Extracting, cleaning, and analyzing datasets using Excel and SQL
  • Creating, maintaining, and enhancing Power BI dashboards
  • Validating data accuracy across reports and data sources
  • Documenting data workflows, assumptions, and reporting logic
  • Collaborating with team members to understand reporting requirements
  • Supporting improvements to reports, processes, and workflows
  • Gaining exposure to VB.NET used in internal systems

While some analytical and problem-solving concepts were introduced through coursework, most learning occurred on the job by working with real business data and collaborating with experienced professionals.

Key Learning and Reflection

One of the most important lessons from this work term was the importance of clear communication. Technical analysis is only valuable if results are accurate, well-documented, and presented in a way that stakeholders can easily understand.

I also gained an appreciation for documentation as a long-term asset. Creating structured, reusable documentation ensures continuity and allows others to build upon existing work after a co-op term ends.

Overall, this experience strengthened my technical confidence, analytical thinking, and professional communication skills.

Conclusion

My co-op work term at Linamar Corporation was a valuable professional experience that allowed me to apply academic knowledge in a real business environment. I developed practical technical skills, contributed to operational reporting, and gained insight into how data supports organizational decision-making.

This experience has reinforced my interest in roles that combine business, data, and technology, and it has prepared me for future work terms and professional opportunities.

Acknowledgments

I would like to thank my manager, team members, and colleagues at Linamar Corporation for their support, guidance, and mentorship throughout my work term. Their willingness to share knowledge played a significant role in my learning and development.
