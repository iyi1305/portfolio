Work Term Report

Business Analyst at Linamar

Abstract/Introduction

During my co-op term at Linamar, I worked as a Business Analyst in the Financial Systems department. My responsibilities spanned financial data analysis, process improvement, stakeholder collaboration, and technical documentation for OneStream, Linamar’s financial performance management tool. This experience allowed me to blend technical skills with business analysis, contributing to data-driven decision-making and operational efficiency.

Information About the Employer

Linamar Corporation is a global pioneer in manufacturing solutions, focussing on automotive powertrain systems, mobility innovation, and industrial products. Linamar, with over 30,000 employees in 60 locations throughout the world, is known for its commitment to industrial quality and sustainability. As a member of the Financial Systems team, I helped to optimise reporting procedures and increase financial performance insights, which aligned with the company's data-driven decision-making strategy.

Goals

  • Technical Proficiency: Gain hands-on experience with financial performance management tools like OneStream and Excel.
  • Business Analysis: Develop skills in requirements gathering, workflow mapping, and creating documentation for end users.
  • Stakeholder Collaboration: Build communication skills to effectively liaise with technical teams and business stakeholders.
  • Problem-Solving: Apply analytical thinking to identify inefficiencies and recommend process improvements.

Reflection on Goals: Successfully mastered OneStream, Excel, and workflow mapping. While I improved communication skills, presenting complex technical solutions to non-technical stakeholders remains a growth area.

Job Description

As a Business Analyst, I worked on various initiatives aimed at improving Linamar’s financial systems and processes:

  • Requirements Gathering: Collaborated with end users to understand reporting needs and translate them into functional requirements for system enhancements.
  • Data Analysis: Analyzed financial data to identify trends, ensure data accuracy, and support decision-making processes.
  • Process Mapping: Documented existing workflows and identified opportunities for automation or optimization.
  • Financial Reporting: Developed and refined reports in OneStream to provide actionable insights for the finance team.
  • Documentation: Authored comprehensive work instructions for OneStream functionalities, ensuring ease of use for future users.

Key Projects/Experiences

1. Optimizing Financial Reporting

One of my key duties was to improve the efficiency and dependability of financial reports generated using OneStream. I worked with stakeholders to identify gaps in existing reports, create custom Cube Views, and automate data validation processes. These changes cut report creation time by 20% and gave the finance team precise insights for strategic decision-making.

2. Workflow and Process Improvement

I examined existing workflows for financial data handling and reporting to identify inefficiencies and opportunities for improvement. Through process mapping and collaboration, I recommended and executed changes to minimise bottlenecks, streamline operations, and reduce manual duties. These advancements boosted productivity and paved the way for future process automation.

3. Documentation for OneStream Functionalities

To promote OneStream adoption, I designed user-friendly instructions for important functionality such as Cube Views and data validation processes. These materials are easy to understand for both technical and non-technical users, resulting in speedier onboarding and less need for direct help. Colleagues reported increased confidence and productivity after using these instructions.

Skills Developed

  • Advanced technical skills in OneStream, Excel, and SQL.
  • Enhanced abilities in requirements gathering and process documentation.
  • Improved verbal and written communication with diverse teams.
  • Strengthened analytical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Reflections and Lessons Learned

I was able to combine my technical knowledge with business analysis in this position, which helped me gain a better grasp of how financial systems help organisations achieve their objectives. My interest in the nexus of business and technology was cemented when I had hands-on experience in requirements gathering, data analysis, and process optimisation.

Acknowledgments

I want to express my gratitude to Jessica Hesch, my supervisor, and the Linamar Financial Systems team for their guidance and assistance during my co-op period. My development as a business analyst was greatly aided by their advice.
