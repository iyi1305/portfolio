Job Description

In my role as a Research Assistant, I focused on analyzing international agreements managed by the OVPR. My responsibilities included collecting and synthesizing data from agreements with France, Germany, and India, and developing detailed reports on trends and insights. Proficiency in Excel for data analysis and reporting was crucial. This role required applying theoretical knowledge from my studies and acquiring practical skills in data management and report generation.

Skills Required: Data analysis, report writing, proficiency in Excel, and information synthesis.

Learning: Gained practical experience in data analysis and reporting, complementing academic knowledge and preparing for future research roles.

Conclusions

My co-op term at OVPR provided invaluable experience in research, data analysis, and communication. The skills developed and the knowledge gained have laid a strong foundation for future roles in research, enhancing my ability to contribute effectively to international collaborations.
